# Audio Resources

To enable the ambient audio feature, you need to add two MP3 files to the `public/sounds/` directory:

1.  `surface.mp3` - For the surface level (wind, waves, light atmosphere).
2.  `deep.mp3` - For the deep level (underwater rumble, sonar, dark atmosphere).

## Where to find free audio?

Here are some great sources for royalty-free sound effects and ambient music:

*   **Pixabay Sound Effects**: [https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/underwater/](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/underwater/)
*   **FreeSound.org**: [https://freesound.org/](https://freesound.org/) (Search for "ocean", "underwater", "drone", "ambient")
*   **Mixkit**: [https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/nature/](https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/nature/)

## Recommended Search Terms

*   **Surface**: "Ocean waves", "Sea breeze", "Beach ambience", "Light wind"
*   **Deep**: "Underwater rumble", "Submarine ambience", "Deep drone", "Dark ambient", "Sonar"
